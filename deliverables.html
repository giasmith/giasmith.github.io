Database

  • We are likely to have 2 main databases for Items and Users
  • The plan is to use the ODU systems group provided databases for our backend data storage using mySQL
  • ITEM(ItemID, Name, Description, Price, ImageLink, Location, RenterID)
  • USER(UserID, FirstName, LastName, ProfileDescription, Rating, Username, Password, Email)
  • We will also provide more databases to facilitate owner-renter communications, such as the following
  • REQUEST(RequestID, ItemID, UserID)
  • RATING(ReviewID, UserID, RaterID, Rating, ReviewText, ReviewType)
  • MESSAGE(MessageID, RequestID, MessageText, MessageSender)
