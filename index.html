Problem Statement

What we are addressing is two problems. People who need an item i.e. (a power washer or a leaf blower), but don’t want or need to buy one outright and people who have that item, don’t want to permanently sell it off, but desire a way to turn unused possessions into passive income. This app would help people in need of renting and help the other make money with an item they don’t need all the time.
